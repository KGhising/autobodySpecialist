Auto Body Specialist

Your one-stop garage

Auto Body Specialist is your one-stop garage in Putalisadak, Kathmandu offering comprehensive services for your vehicle. Our garage is located on one of the main bus routes, from cribbs causeway to the city centre. Established in 2005, Auto Body Specialist carries out any kind of car servicing and repair work to the highest standards.

We have 30 years of experience as car mechanics, so you can rely on us for a quality service. We can work on any vehicle, whatever the make or model, and can source original manufacturer parts should it be required. Auto Body Specialist provides a 12 month guarantee on any part installed. Contact us today to discuss your requirements and for a quote.
